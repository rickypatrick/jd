Swedish Massage

A Swedish massage is the most common type of massage that uses several different techniques to help the body relax and decompress from muscle tension and soreness. It helps with blood circulation, decrease stress and, relieves muscle tightness.

Deep Tissue

Deep tissue massage is a massage technique involving slow strokes using direct pressure or friction movements to break up adhesions in the muscle tissue. This technique targets muscles below the body's surface and is used with chronic pain and athletes.

Reflexology

Reflexology is a practice applying different amounts of pressure to different points on the hands and feet. These different points are believed to represent different parts of the body; and by manipulating these points, you can improve the health of the body.

Thai Massage

Thai massage or Thai yoga massage combines yogic stretches with acupressure. The therapist manipulates the body in different yoga poses while pressing and kneading the muscles with their hands and feet The client wears loose fitting clothing and oil/ lotion is not used.

Benefits of Massage Therapy

Feel Better

Massage therapy offers a myriad of benefits that contribute to both physical and mental well-being. Through skilled manipulation of soft tissues and muscles, massage therapy promotes relaxation, reduces stress, and alleviates tension, ultimately leading to a heightened sense of relaxation and improved mood. Beyond its relaxation effects, massage therapy can enhance circulation, helping to deliver oxygen and nutrients to cells while aiding in the removal of waste products. This can result in improved muscle function and reduced inflammation, making it particularly valuable for athletes and individuals recovering from injuries.

Additionally, regular massage sessions have been shown to lower blood pressure, boost immune system function, and enhance flexibility. Whether seeking relief from chronic pain, aiming to enhance overall wellness, or simply seeking a rejuvenating experience, massage therapy stands as a holistic approach to achieving a healthier mind and body.
